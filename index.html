About Elixir '23

It is an inimitable product of Kolkata's unique love for culture and BPPIMT's proverbial tendency towards excellence. It is a week long extravaganza with equal parts of splendour in organization and turnout. This is not only a mere fest for the students of our college but also an emotion as we all indulge ourselves and invite our friends to make it the most beautiful festival in the crest of Kolkata. We all hope that every heart connects with us and vibes with the colours of Elixir to make it bigger. We are young, wild and exuberant. Come join us as we celebrate and indulge in the various joys of life.
